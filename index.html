About The Company

Gautam Infrastructure; Construction Equipments Private Limited, established in 2006, is built on a legacy of expertise dating back to 1981.

With over four decades of experience, we are a leading provider of work-at-height solutions, specializing in Mast Climbing Work Platforms (MCWP) across India.

We are not just a rental company—we are a solution provider delivering customized access systems designed to enhance safety, efficiency, and productivity on every project.

Our equipment complies with CE and ANSI international safety standards, ensuring world-class reliability and safety.

Mast Climbing Work Platforms (MCWP)

Our Mast Climbing Work Platform (MCWP) systems utilize a heavy-duty Rack & Pinion drive mechanism, providing a safe, stable, and highly efficient alternative to traditional scaffolding.

Designed for high-performance applications, our platforms ensure superior productivity, safety, and ease of operation across a wide range of projects.

Technical Specifications
  • Platform Length: 4.1 m – 19 m
  • Working Height: 300+ meters
  • Drive System: Rack and Pinion
  • Power Supply: 3-Phase, 400 V, 50 Hz
  • Front Extension: 0.5 m – 7 m
  • Load Capacity: Up to 4,700 kg (single mast configuration)
Applications:
  • Construction of High-Rise Structures
  • Maintenance & Inspection
  • Demolition Projects
  • Shutdown Jobs
  • Façade Installation
